Cooking: The Hobby That Keeps You Alive

Health Benefits

Cooking at home allows you to prioritize fresh ingredients, fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins, which contribute to a more balanced diet.

Cost Savings

Eating out or ordering takeout frequently can be expensive. Cooking at home is typically much more affordable, especially when buying ingredients in bulk and planning meals.

Independence

Knowing how to cook makes you more independent, as you don't need to rely on others or restaurants for your meals.
